Privacy Policy

CasHIA - Your Trusted Earning Companion

Effective Date: March 28, 2026 | Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Welcome to CasHIA! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for CasHIA, we collect:

  • Email Address - Used for account creation and login
  • Username - Your display name in the app
  • Avatar Selection - Your chosen profile picture
  • Account Creation Date - When you joined CasHIA

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your activity:

  • Game Statistics - Ads watched, scratch cards played, game scores
  • Coin Balance - Your current and total earned coins
  • Login Streak - Your daily login activity
  • Achievements - Unlocked achievements and progress
  • Leaderboard Rankings - Your position on daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards

Device Information

  • Device ID - Unique device identifier
  • Device Model - Make and model of your device
  • Operating System - OS version
  • App Version - Version of CasHIA you're using

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your earnings and game progress
  • To display leaderboard rankings
  • To award achievements and bonuses
  • To improve our app and user experience
  • To send push notifications about game updates and rewards
  • To prevent fraud and ensure fair play
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services, which employ industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

CasHIA uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, and analytics GDPR Compliant
  • IronSource - Ad serving and monetization GDPR Compliant
  • Google Play Services - App distribution and updates

These services may collect information about your device and usage. They have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Advertising

CasHIA uses IronSource to serve video ads. These ads may use:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • General location (country level only)

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (Limit Ad Tracking on iOS or Opt Out of Ads Personalization on Android).

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days. Game statistics and leaderboard data may be anonymized and retained for analytical purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

CasHIA is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time by contacting our support team. Upon account deletion:

  • All personal data will be permanently removed
  • Your coins and progress will be lost
  • Your leaderboard entries will be removed
  • This action cannot be undone
